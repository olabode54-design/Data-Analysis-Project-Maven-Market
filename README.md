## Executive Dashboard
The Executive Dashboard provides leadership with real-time visibility into revenue growth, profitability trends, return rates, and brand-level performance risks.

![Exec Dashboard](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f7b01b40-ea5d-4dc4-9a0d-087e99ea4d15)

## Key Performance Indicators
Monthly Transactions: 18,325 (+5.69% MoM growth)
Monthly Profit: $71,682.4
Monthly Returns: 496
Revenue vs Target: $120.2K achieved vs $119.5K target for the latest month. 

## Strategic Insights
Positive month-over-month growth indicates expanding customer activity and stable demand.
Revenue performance is closely aligned with monthly targets, suggesting reliable forecasting and demand planning.
The USA contributes the majority share of revenue, highlighting regional dependency and potential expansion opportunities in Canada and Mexico.
The King and Genteel brands exhibit the lowest profitability and the highest return rates across the portfolio.

## Customer Dashboard 
The Customer Dashboard provides deeper visibility into who is driving revenue and how customer demographics influence purchasing patterns.

## Key Customer Metrics
Total Customers: 8.8K
Revenue per Customer: $199.6
Top Revenue-Contributing Customer: Ida Rodriguez
Store with Highest Customer Concentration: Store 7
Most Ordered Product: Moms Roasted Chicken (225 units sold) for the latest month.

## Strategic Customer Insights
A relatively small group of top customers contributes disproportionately to revenue, indicating dependency on high-value segments.
Balanced Gender Revenue Distribution Revenue contribution is nearly evenly split across gender, demonstrating broad market appeal.
Customers in professional and skilled occupations drive the highest transaction volumes, indicating a potentially premium or convenience-driven customer base.
Store 7 leads in customer concentration, presenting opportunities for localized marketing optimization and inventory scaling.
High-volume products such as Moms Roasted Chicken indicate clear demand anchors that can be leveraged for bundling and cross-selling strategies.

## Final Conclusion/recommendation
- Brands with elevated return rates such as King and Genteel should be investigated to improve product quality or customer satisfaction.
- James Bay brand has the lowest return rate and a reasonable profit margin. This could be worked on to drive revenue.
- Loyalty strategies targeting high-value customers should be developed
- Optimize inventory for top-performing items such as Moms Roasted Chicken.
- Include products or develop marketing strategies that will appeal to non-parents.
